Getting Started

Welcome to TimeWarden! This guide will walk you through setting up your server for the first time, mapping roles, and preparing your system to track time.

Quick Setup Formula

Invite Bot → Login to Dashboard → Map Roles → You're Done!

1. Invite the Bot

Ensure the bot has administrator permissions in your Discord server. This allows it to create necessary logs, manage roles if required, and read message commands reliably.

2. Role Mapping (Crucial)

TimeWarden does not track everyone. It only tracks users with designated Employee Roles.

  • Admin Roles: Members with these roles can login to the dashboard and configure settings.
  • Employee Roles: Members with these roles can use the /clock command. They cannot change server settings.
[Placeholder: Screenshot of the "Admin Roles" and "Employee Roles" mapping screen in the dashboard]

3. Testing the System

Once your roles are mapped, jump into any channel where the bot has access and type:

/clock in

You should receive a prompt confirming your shift has started. Your time is now being tracked!

Employee Workflows

TimeWarden provides multiple avenues for your staff to interact with the system seamlessly.

Discord Slash Commands

The standard way to log time is directly through Discord.

  • /clock in - Starts a shift.
  • /clock out - Ends a shift and calculates hours.
  • /clock status - Privately shows your current shift duration.
[Placeholder: GIF showing an employee using the /clock command]

The Web Dashboard

Employees can log into the web dashboard to access their personal profiles. Here they can:

  • View their entire shift history and total hours.
  • Request Time Adjustments if they forgot to clock in or out.
  • Clock in/out from the web interface if they aren't near Discord.
[Placeholder: Screenshot of the Employee Personal Profile dashboard view]

Tablet Kiosk Mode

Business Tier Feature

Kiosk Mode requires the Business Tier subscription.

For physical workplaces (like restaurants or brick-and-mortar stores), Admins can mount a tablet and open the Kiosk Mode URL. Employees simply tap their profile picture and enter a 4-digit PIN to clock in, completely circumventing Discord.

Exports & Payroll

Handling payroll should be a one-click process. TimeWarden simplifies data extraction so you can accurately compensate your team.

Generating Timesheets

Navigate to the Reports & Exports tab in the dashboard. You can export data over any custom date range. Note: Free tier users can only generate reports for the last 24 hours.

[Placeholder: Screenshot of the Reports & Exports configuration screen]

Export Formats

Format Description Tier Required
Standard CSV A basic, raw data export of all shifts, durations, and employee names. Pro (for >24h)
Quickbooks / Gusto CSV Pre-formatted CSV designed explicitly to be imported directly into payroll software without manual spreadsheet shifting. Pro
Branded PDF A stylized document ideal for presenting to clients or filing offline records. Pro

Automated Email Reports

Instead of manually logging in, you can configure TimeWarden to automatically email you a Daily or Weekly summary of all clocked hours. Configure this in the Settings tab.

Pricing & Tiers

TimeWarden scales with your community or business. We offer three simple tiers.

Free Tier

Designed for testing and extremely small teams. You get full access to the bot, but with structural constraints.

  • 3 Employee Hard-Cap: Only 3 employees can be actively tracked.
  • 24-Hour History: You cannot view or export data older than 24 hours.

The 30-Day Premium Trial

All new servers automatically receive 30 days of Pro Tier features so you can fully test the system without limits before committing.

Pro Tier ($9/mo)

The standard choice for most online communities and digital teams.

  • Unlimited Employees: Track as many people as you need.
  • Unlimited History: Export data spanning an entire year.
  • Advanced Payroll: Unlock Quickbooks/Gusto formatting and PDF exports.

Business Tier ($19/mo)

For physical storefronts or advanced organizational setups.

  • Everything in Pro
  • Tablet Kiosk Mode: Allow physical clock-ins via PIN pad.
  • Priority Support
